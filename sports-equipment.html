Sports Equipment Program

Recreation equipment is not supplied by the Department of Corrections. Saint Dismas Prison Ministry purchases footballs, soccer balls, basketballs, nets, rims, orange cones, colored pinneys, time clocks, stop watches, etc., and has them shipped from the supplier directly to the prison warehouse in care of the Prison Chaplain.
