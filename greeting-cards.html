Greeting Card Program

Prison inmates who are serving long sentences often lose family ties and meaningful friendships over the decades that they are incarcerated. Many never ever receive mail.

Recognizing the need to encourage communication and to help make it possible, the Saint Dismas Foundation was instrumental in starting a free greeting card program many years ago. Since it's inception well over 100,000 cards have been provided free to any inmate that requests them from the Chaplain.
