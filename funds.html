Indigent Inmate Funds

The Foundation makes direct deposits to the Inmate Trust Fund in the name of qualified indigent inmates (usually $10) to enable them to purchase personal hygeiene supplies (deoderant, toothpaste, etc.) which are not supplied by the prison.
