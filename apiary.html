Apiary Program (Bee Keeping)

A re-entry program proposed by the Saint Dismas Foundation to teach inmates how to make a living in a $4 billion a year market. It is a direct benefit to the Farm program, increasing crop yields. We estimate 400 lbs. of honey in the first year. The Foundation finances the project.
